Is recycling important?
Recycling is very important as waste has a huge negative impact on the natural environment. Harmful chemicals and greenhouse gasses are released from rubbish in landfill sites. Recycling helps to reduce the pollution caused by waste.
2 / 2
How much waste do we generate per day in Malaysia?
Over 23,000 tonnes of waste is produced each day in Malaysia. The amount of waste generated continues to increase due to the increasing population and development, and only less than 5% of the waste is being recycled.
