EARTH WARRIOR 2019: Make the Earth Cleaner
Venue: Taman Bunga Raya     Date: 9am, Nov 07-09, 2019

Organised by Students of Sunway University

In conjunction with the Sustainable Goals DEvelopment, we, students from Sunway University, would like to make our efford to help make the Earth a better place. Join us in our event to help clean the rubbish around the parks and roads! You can make a difference!
