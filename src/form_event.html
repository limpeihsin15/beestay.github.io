What event are acceptted?

Here are some objectives of a recycle event:

  • To reduce its impact on the environment
  • To help increase environmental awareness
  • To create goodwill amongst the team staging the event
  • To meet increasing community expectation to be seen to be green
  • To streamline recycling activities and strategize efforts to increase recycling rate
